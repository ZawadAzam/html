Cricket World Cup 2023

Maxwell Did Double Century

The all-rounder came to the crease with his side 49-4, which soon became 91-7, in pursuit of 292 to seal a semi-final spot. Maxwell's innings, the highest men's one-day international score by an Australia batter, was all the more remarkable given he battled cramp and back spasms throughout. He joined Kate Cross and Alex Hartley on No Balls: The Cricket Podcast to talk through his astonishing knock.
